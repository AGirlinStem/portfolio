Functional Wind Turbine


This wind turbine is made using popsicle sticks, a glue gun, a balloon, clay, and an index card. The base is a square with rubber at the bottom for extra stability, and clay is used to make it more solid. The four supporting pillars are slightly different heights: the front ones are 24 cm, and the back ones are 25.5 cm. The front pillars are straight, and the back ones are angled, with cross braces for added support.

The turbine blades are made from four thin popsicle sticks and are flexible, allowing them to change angles. These blades are connected to the motor with another popsicle stick, inspired by the design of wind turbines and high-voltage towers.
