About me

I am a second-year Electrical Engineering student at the University of Windsor, currently working as a Teaching Assistant for the Cornerstone Design course, where I assist with programming and design. I'm also a member of the Formula SAE Electric team, focusing on PCB design, and serve as the undergraduate Electrical Engineering student representative for the Appointment Committee. In addition to my academic and team responsibilities, I am continuously learning and exploring new technologies, including 3D printing and other innovative skills in engineering and design. I've always had a passion for problem-solving, and pursuing a career in the field of engineering is giving me the skill sets to turn those solutions into reality. I am passionate about encouraging more people, especially women, to pursue careers in STEM, as I believe diversity in engineering leads to stronger, more creative solutions and a more inclusive future for all.
